Our History

“Ulta Pizza” the brainchild of entrepreneur Mr.Umesh Shah, who has over a decade of experience inventing new delicious authentic vegetarian treats for the discerning public.While experimenting with different food combination few years backhe came up with now popular & hot favourite of many “Ulta Pizza,” and coined the name on the basis of its method of preparation. Likewise at “Ulta Pizza” we have many such mouth-watering delicious treats such as Double Decker Pizza, Arrabiota, Nachos, Mix Pasta, Tawa Pulav, Cheese Chilli Grill Sandwich, Cheese Dosa Sezwan, Khada Pav Bhaji, Butter Scotch Milk Shake etc. “Ulta Pizza”dining scene features a rich mix of local street fare as well as unique and quality dining experiences.

The food at "Ulta Pizza" is something that really stands out. The food is scrumptious and the service zippy. Our offerings are damn succumbing and perfect in flavour & quality.Everything is fresh and is with amazing flavour and texture, and the spices just right. All in all a Favourite fast food joint serving tasty savouries to our customers at reasonable rate.We highly recommend you trying several different things. Whether you want to celebrate an occasion with your friends, colleagues or loved ones, “Ulta Pizza” is just the place.Come, discover the joy of partying well.
