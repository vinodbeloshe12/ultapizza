WEL COME TO ULTA PIZZA

"Ulta Pizza" is renowned name in the fast food world of south Mumbai. It is a Pure Veg. outlet which serve Varieties of Pizza, & Sandwich and various fast food savouries like Pav Bhaji, South Indian snacks,Chinese & Continental Cuisines,North Indian Pulao, Kababs, Tikkis, Korma, etc.,and Varieties of Milk Shakes & Fresh Fruit Juices. Ulta Pizza is also good for a quick bite to eat. We have the fresh, piping hot and Delicious Ulta Pizza, Pinky Pizza, Cheese Grill Sandwich, Arrabiotta& Alfredo Pasta, Chhole Tikki, Nachos, Rolls, Masala Pav,Paneer Frankie,Dabeli, and street food from various regions in India with the flavours spot on. The secret for the success of the Ulta Pizza is the quick tasty bite one gets which is also easy on their wallet. Ulta Pizza is also a popular hangout joints for office goers, college students or for that matter to anyone for a quick bite.Ulta Pizza is definitely for the tongue as it caters primarily to our baser instincts and is a levelling factor where a sea of humanity congregates to fine tune their taste buds. Ulta Pizza fast food is also the favourite take away food joints for many of our patrons.
